Frequently Asked Questions

What is an RFI?

A Request for Information (RFI) is a formal question from a contractor to an engineer or architect seeking clarification on drawings, specifications, or other project documentation. SiteStream digitizes this process.

How does AI improve RFI responses?

SiteStream uses AI in three ways: Voice-to-RFI lets you speak an RFI on site and AI converts it into a structured request. Smart Photo Analysis auto-describes site issues from photos you attach. And Plain-English Translation converts complex engineering jargon so contractors and engineers actually understand each other.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and role-based access controls. Your project data is never shared with third parties.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! Our Free plan lets you manage 1 project with 2 team members at no cost. Upgrade when you need more capacity.
